# 📊 Loan Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer loan data to identify high-risk customers and understand key factors influencing loan defaults. The analysis was performed using EDA, SQL, and Power BI to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify high-risk customers  
- Analyze the impact of income, profession, and demographics on loan risk  
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (Data extraction & transformation)  
- EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis)  
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)  
- DAX (Calculated columns & measures)  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- 250,000+ customer records  
- Features include:  
  - Income  
  - Age  
  - Experience  
  - Profession  
  - Marital Status  
  - State  
  - Risk Flag (Target variable)  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards:
  - Total Customers  
  - Risk Percentage  
  - Average Income  
  - Average Experience  

- Visualizations:
  - Risk Distribution (Pie Chart)  
  - Risk by Income Group  
  - Top 10 High-Risk Professions  
  - Top 10 States by Risk Level  
  - Risk Comparison by Marital Status  

- Interactive Filters:
  - State  
  - Profession  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- ~8.13% of customers are high-risk  
- Lower-income customers show higher default risk  
- Certain professions have consistently higher risk levels  
- Risk varies across different states  
- Single customers are slightly more risky than married customers  

---

## 💡 Conclusion
Income level is the most influential factor affecting loan risk, followed by demographic factors such as profession and marital status. These insights can help financial institutions improve credit decision-making and reduce default risk.
